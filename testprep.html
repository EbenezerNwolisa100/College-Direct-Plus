What Is Test Prep and Tutoring?

Test prep and tutoring are educational services provided by College Direct Plus that help students improve their grades and prepare for admissions exams. At College Direct Plus, our tutors are subject-matter experts with decades of experience helping students achieve their grade and test score goals.

Academic excellence is necessary, but not sufficient on its own to gain admission to the most selective schools. Holistic admissions also place increasing importance on an applicant’s soft factors, as schools are seeking students who will contribute to the community, add to the diversity, and who have explored and pursued their academic interests independently.

Academic Tutoring

From helping students build foundational academic skills that carry across all subjects, to supporting subject matter expertise, our expert tutors help students with every facet of their educational goals including:

Math and Science

Algebra, Geometry, Trigonometry, Calculus, Biology, Chemistry, Physics, Statistics, Computer Science

Language

Spanish, French, Italian, German, Latin

Humanities

U.S. History, World History, European History, Writing and Composition, Government and Politics, Economics, Psychology, Classics

Academic Skills

Executive Functioning, Study Skills, Time Management, Organizational Skills, Critical Reading, Expository Writing, ESL Support

Test Prep

Despite many colleges and universities going test-optional, standardized testing remains an important factor in admissions, and our test prep tutors have vast experience with standardized tests including:

College Entrance Exams

SAT, ACT, PSAT, AP Exams, TOEFL

Graduate School Entrance Exams

GMAT, LSAT, MCAT, GRE

Elementary and High School Entrance Exams

ISEE, SSAT, SHSAT
